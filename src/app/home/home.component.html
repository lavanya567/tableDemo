We are pleased to announce that “30TH ANNUAL SBAOI MEETING & INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON BIOMEDICAL MATERIALS INNOVATION-2020 (ICBMI-2020)” will be held from 6/12/2020 to 9/12/2020

The conference theme will be "Biomedical Materials Innovations" a challenge for our society to pursue innovation in biomedical materials in partnership with emerging technology to build future therapeutic hybrid products and functionalities.

We request the honour of your presence at the inaugural ceremony of ICBMI-2020 on 06 Dec 2020 at IST of 18:00 hrs and C. P. Sharma award plenary lecture at IST of 18:30 hrs. The Conference will take place in the virtual environment due to the unforeseen COVID-19 pandemic. We are looking forward to welcoming you
